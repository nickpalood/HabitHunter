Mobile Instructions

  1. Open Revolut
  2. Sign in
  3. Press on "More"
  4. Press on "Statement"
  5. Press on "Excel"
  6. Choose the period (longer is preferable)
  7. Press on "Generate"
  8. Save it to your phone
  9. Send it to your computer
  10. Press "Choose file" above
  11. Submit the file
